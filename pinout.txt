1. 16x2 LCD (I2C):
    SDA -> SDA (2)
    SCL -> SCL (3)

2. DS3231 RTC (I2C):
    SDA -> SDA (2)
    SCL -> SCL (3)
    SQW -> 7
    32K -> - (not used)

3. buzzer: 4 (through 200 Ohm resistor)

4. candle LED: 5 (through 320 Ohm resistor)

5. failure LED: 9 (through 320 Ohm resistor)

6. diagnostics button: 8

7. audio sensor:
    data -> A1
